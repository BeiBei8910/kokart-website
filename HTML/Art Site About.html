About Author

Hello,
My name is Aydin, I’m a freelance artist born and raised in Trinidad and Tobago. I’ve been doing art and illustration since 2013, and most of my skills were developed through self-teaching methods.

About Kokart

Kokart was a site I had developed intending to allow my users to be able to quickly access my portfolio, purchase my content, find out more information about me, and give them a more centralized platform making it easier to contact me about requests or questions they have.
The Home Page allows viewers to see content updates and notices added to the site, the Showcase displays a preview of my works, the Shop allows users to purchase content; in About, users may find out more information about the site, and in Contact is where you may ask questions or request commissions.
